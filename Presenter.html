The Presenter

Engaging Audiences, On Screen and On Stage.

From Cockpit to Camera

After the serious powerboat racing accident in 2010 which put me on the sidelines for an indefinite time, the media world jumped at the chance to get me back on television and radio – this time as an expert pundit for the sport I love so much. This was perfect for me as I was suffering badly from my injuries and mentally could not race for the foreseeable future; I could now support my sport on another dimension.

My natural ability to talk to the press whenever needed has led to great jobs as a television presenter. I enjoy presenting for Sky Sports, CBS America and love my own BBC Radio Show, the H2O Show, which I co-host with Sir Robin Knox Johnston. In 2017, I took on a new venture where I take my commentating skills to Live Streaming social media to promote the National UK Thundercat Racing Series and many other racing series across the world.

Speaker & Event Host

Throughout my racing career, I've not been afraid to stand up and share my thrills and spills, which I do with a great deal of passion on the speaker’s circuit. I bring energy and professionalism to after-dinner talks, award ceremonies, and motivational events.
